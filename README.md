# Business-Case-Generator
Insight:
    After i have worked as a professional innovator for many years I can see corporate understanding lacks for uncertainties.
    This means often ideas gets cancled or down prioritized, my assumption is due to misunderstandings or lack of decision power.
    To mitigate this we have seen that visual concepts and storytelling together with a business case gives a better match between innovation and corporate language.

Problem:
    To create a usable business case you need a lot of knowledge about an idea. All the typical templates is more for classic conventional project management, which does not match the creative methods in the innovaiton space. To create the bridge takes a lot of time and effort, and every time we generate data we use a lot of energy which is wasted when we know we need to generate 100-300 ideas to be able to test 10-30 that hopefully can generate 1-3 new business opportunities. The more time you spend on the Business cases the more in love with the idea you get and the harder it is to kill the idea. But on the other hand it is a strong tool to create some good business cases to evaluate from and document your journey.

Motivation:
    I have generated a lot of data and tested Ideas. After we let go we forget or let go of knowledge. The process takes a lot of energy for me personally and the same amount of energy is let go of my body when we yet again let go of a potentially good idea. It is getting harder to start a new process the more ideas is put into the grave. Furthermore sometimes I have seen ideas resurfaces from others and with the distance from the last idea we cannot use knowledge from the past. So bottom line we need to generate business cases at the maturity stage we are in with an idea and file it for later use. It should take 6 weeks to go through the process from idea to business case, but lack of motivation and no structure let ideas drag out for months. I need to remove time and effectively execute more to generate more value. But I do not want to tell anyone about how, I want to work less while creating more.

Inspiration:
    AI is upon us and I have seen so much crap output and different styles that I end up still creating a lot of manual elements, especially for Business Cases. But recently I saw a presentation of a product from Virsabi Jens, we had a F2F where he explained how he created tools and apps within hours that would have taken weeks for a programmer. The so called Vibe Coding. It is spaghetty code and dangerous if you do not clean up before scaling solutions, but what if it was not for others but my self. What if I was the inputgiver and the quality control. That got me on the track of my idea. His approach was the same as he did when directing developpers, he cannot code so he explains and let the developpers have freedom to create within a scope and some boundaries. So I tried to talk to Claude.

Ideal Customer:
    The solution is for my own optimization, so no one besides me are allowed to use the solution. So the ideal customer is the ones I generate business cases from. So how do we generarte good enough business cases so leaders can take good decisions if they shall chose to develop a business idea. So the ideal customer is responsible for a team or a SME that needs to understand the business case and feel that they can trust it so they can decide which direction to move without getting too surpriced by the outcome.

Solution:
    Create a business model generator with Claude. It shall work in a way where I give input from text or files, then it shall evaluate if I need to interview idea hero, budget responsible or a partner to generating missing information. Further more indicate if data is factual, assuming or unknown in the business case. I want to speed the normal generation of a business model up so it takes a full week to generate first version, but the speed with AI shall help me use 4 hours per case. I do not want to tell about my solution, nether add new work to my calendar. But if I can generate a business case on a week I am 6 times quicker than estimated, that in it self is value added to Semco, the rest is time added to me. Furthermore the model shall be used for Blue Trampoline.

Value Proposition:
    A tool to generate Business Cases for Idea Heros and their Leaders that helps understand the business potential and therefore make better and more development decisions, in comparision to misunderstandings and uncertainty about true future value.

5-year vision:
    This will be one of many small tools that will help clients from idea to business development. Specificly this tool will be something everyone can do in 5 years, but the trick from me is that I will learn from feedback and create a better tool. It is not the AI it self but what I give, what it asks for and what we can file at my clients. In 5 years there should be many business cases created from the tool and each is a slot in the lottery where they contact for further development.

Business Model:
    I help SME generate ideas, prioritize and pick a couple they want to continue with. I then get paid for creating the business cases from interviews and data where each case costs my time + use of tool. Money is made from hours interviewing and testing + a pricetag calculated on how long time I would use to generate the case. It will cost me a subscription for Claude or it even might run on my laptop. If we say each case would be 20k and each customer would choose 5 ideas I could create, then from each workshop I would generate 100k on the backend plus maybe 25k on the workshop. Some of the business cases will generate new tasks, but lets not take those in. Lets say I do 8 workshops a year which will generate around 1 million a year on that work.
